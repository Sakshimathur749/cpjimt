Centre of Excellence

Our Vision & Mission

The Institute aims to be a Centre of Excellence promoting value based Quality Education in the emerging areas of professional studies in Information Technology & Management.

Vision

To consistently work for improving the institutional perseverance, persistence and patience through excellence in teaching-learning, entrepreneurship and leadership for ensuring continually rich, value-based and globalized career and lifestyles for all students who enroll themselves in the academic programmes conducted at this College.

The Institute aims to be a Centre of Excellence promoting value based Quality Education in the emerging areas of professional studies in Information Technology & Management.

Mission

Market-Focused Programmes

To introduce market focused professional programmes in emerging areas of higher education in all such disciplines which have high job potential, namely, Management, Commerce, Computer Applications & Law etc.
