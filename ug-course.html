Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) at CPJ Institute of Management & Technology (CPJIMT) is a 4-year undergraduate program designed to create future-ready business professionals. The program delivers a strong foundation in Management, Marketing, finance, human resources, and entrepreneurship, combined with industry-oriented specializations that enhance employability and leadership potential.

Program Highlights

Practical & Experiential Learning

Hands-on learning through live projects & case studies, industry internships, business simulations, and workshops and management games.

Industry-Focused Curriculum

The BBA curriculum at CPJIMT blends core business knowledge with real-world applications, enabling students to understand how organizations function in today's dynamic business environment.

Soft Skills & Personality Development

These essential skills give CPJIMT students a competitive edge in the job market.

  • Communication & presentation skills
  • Leadership & team management
  • Problem-solving & decision-making
  • Corporate etiquette & professionalism

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed Class XII (10+2) from a recognised board
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks

Why Choose BBA from CPJIMT?

Recognised among the best BBA colleges in Delhi NCR, CPJIMT focuses on practical exposure, skill development, and assured career opportunities.

Assured Placements & Internships

CPJIMT offers 100% placement and internship assistance. Our dedicated placement cell collaborates with leading companies and corporate partners.

Value-Added Programs

Expert lectures by industry professionals, skill-based workshops and seminars, national & international conferences, and educational and industrial visits.

World-Class Infrastructure

Smart classrooms with advanced teaching tools, well-equipped computer and management labs, digital learning resources and libraries.

World-Class Infrastructure

A professional academic environment that supports innovation and creativity.

Marketing Executive

Sales Executive

Financial Analyst

Plus: The program also lays a strong foundation for MBA, entrepreneurship, and higher studies.
