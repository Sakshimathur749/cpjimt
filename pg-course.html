Master of Laws (LL.M)

CPJIMT's Master of Laws (LL.M) is a 2-year postgraduate law program designed to provide advanced and specialized legal education to law graduates. The program focuses on deep legal understanding, research excellence, and professional legal development.

Preparing students for careers in higher legal practice, judiciary, academia, corporate law, and public policy. Recognised among the best LL.M colleges in Delhi NCR, CPJIMT ensures a strong blend of academic rigor and practical legal insight.

Program Highlights

Advanced Legal Knowledge

The LL.M program at CPJIMT helps students gain:

  • In-depth understanding of constitutional principles and jurisprudence
  • Advanced statutory interpretation and legal reasoning
  • Specialised expertise in selected areas of law
  • Strong analytical and argumentative skills

Research & Practical Learning

Students gain hands-on exposure through:

  • Research methodology and dissertation work
  • Case law analysis and academic seminars
  • Legal writing, presentations, and discussions
  • Continuous evaluation through research-oriented activities

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completed LL.B or equivalent from a recognised university
  • Secured at least 50% aggregate marks, as per applicable norms
