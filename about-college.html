About the College

CPJ Institute of Management & Technology (CPJIMT) is a prestigious institution and a proud constituent of the renowned CPJ Group of Institutions, carrying a rich academic legacy of over 22 years. Over the years, CPJIMT has emerged as a symbol of excellence in higher education, committed to nurturing future-ready professionals and responsible citizens.

The primary objective of CPJIMT is to deliver quality, value-based education while actively contributing to the growth and advancement of the Indian Higher Education Ecosystem. The institute emphasizes academic excellence, professional competence, ethical values, and holistic student development.

With a strong academic foundation, CPJIMT is supported by a dedicated team of more than 100+ academic and non-academic staff members, who work collectively to provide a stimulating and student-centric learning environment. The institute believes in empowering students through a blend of theoretical knowledge, practical exposure, and industry-oriented learning.

The CPJ Group of Institutions takes pride in its robust placement ecosystem, supported by a strong network of 100+ reputed recruiters actively participating in campus placement drives. This sustained effort has resulted in over 5,000+ placement offers, enabling students to smoothly transition from academia to professional careers.

CPJIMT also boasts a vibrant alumni network of 7,000+ successful professionals, excelling across diverse sectors. Their achievements reflect the institute's commitment to developing individuals who demonstrate not only technical expertise but also integrity, leadership, and ethical values.

Commitment to Excellence

CPJIMT aspires to be recognized as a Centre of Excellence in technical and professional education. The institute focuses on holistic development by fostering academic competence, professional ethics, leadership qualities, and social responsibility. Through a supportive and inspiring learning environment, CPJIMT equips students with the skills and knowledge required to excel in their chosen fields and contribute positively to society and the nation.

Objectives

To offer industry-oriented academic programmes aligned with current and future job market requirements.

To inculcate ethical values, integrity, and social responsibility among students.

To actively engage in initiatives that support inclusive growth and community development, with a focus on marginalized and disadvantaged sections of society.

Committed to delivering quality higher and professional education through academic excellence.
